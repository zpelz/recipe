Dori's Barbecued Salmon Steak

(serves two)

  • 1 tbsp. unhulled sesame seeds
  • 3 tbsp. medium dry sherry
  • 1 tbsp. dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Oriental sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp. fresh minced ginger
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 2 medium garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 salmon steaks, cut about 1"thick
    (1/2 lb each) (or halibut)
  1. Combine sesame seeds, sherry, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, scallions, and garlic in a bowl to make marinade.
  2. Place fish on rack of grill - baste at intervals.
Can also be broiled.
  1. Preheat oven to broil for 20 minutes.
  2. Place salmon steaks in a flame poof dish with a lid.
  3. Pour marinade over salmon and let it stand while the oven is heating.
  4. Broil for 10 minutes without turning, serve immediately.
